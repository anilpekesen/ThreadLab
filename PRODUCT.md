# Product

## Register

product

## Users

PrintLab operators, Shopify merchants, and assigned partners use the product while managing personalized print orders, store performance, subscriptions, support, and production files.

## Product Purpose

PrintLab is a Shopify product personalization and print production app. It helps merchants collect customer designs, prepare print files, track usage and orders, and lets internal admins monitor stores, subscriptions, support, and partner commissions.

## Brand Personality

Practical, precise, trustworthy. The interface should feel like an operations tool that makes decisions clear and keeps sensitive store data scoped to the right person.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-style dashboards, decorative cards, vague metrics, playful visual treatments, and partner views that expose stores or admin tools beyond their assignment.

## Design Principles

- Keep operational data dense but readable.
- Make role boundaries obvious between admin, merchant, and partner.
- Prefer direct controls and status labels over explanatory copy.
- Surface financial totals with clear assumptions.
- Preserve existing workflows when adding management features.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use clear contrast, standard form controls, keyboard-friendly actions, and text labels that are understandable without color alone.
